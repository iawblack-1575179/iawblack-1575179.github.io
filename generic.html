Kim Jong Un Facts

01.01.2017

  • In order to humble himself, Kim Jong Un invented the teleprompter to fake reading his speeches off of it. Glorious leader memorizes all of his speeches in his head.
  • When the North Korean basketball team was losing by 20 points at the Olympic Games, the spectating Kim Jong Un subbed himself in. The North Korean team won by 100 points.
  • Kim Jong Un is well known for his love of Disney. For 10 years he played Mickey Mouse at Tokyo Disneyland without anyone noticing!
  • When the North Korean stock market began to start performing poorly, Kim Jong Un ordered every single North Korean to take a week off work and that he would handle it. A week later the market saw its best performance in history.
  • After the famous actor Jackie Chan injured himself doing one of his own stunts, Kim Jong Un offered to become his stunt double. The movie studio was so blown away by Kim Jong Un's stunts that he is now the stunt double in every Jackie Chan movie. They even re-filmed the stunts in Chan's old films with Kim's.
